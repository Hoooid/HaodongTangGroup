Our students presented recent research at EMP 2025 in Macau.


<!--more-->

More than ten students from our group participated in the International Conference on Energy, Materials, and Photonics 2025 (EMP25), held in Macau from August 8 to 10, 2025. The conference brought together researchers, journal editors, and industry professionals working across optoelectronic materials, energy technologies, semiconductors, and photonics.

Hosted collaboratively by Macau University of Science and Technology, the Institute of Nanoscience and Applications, Southern University of Science and Technology, Society for Energy Photonics, Optica Shenzhen Section, and the IEEE Nanotechnology Council Guangdong Chapter, the conference featured a robust program of plenary talks, keynote addresses, invited lectures, and oral presentations.

Our students delivered oral presentations and joined discussions with researchers from related fields. Their presentations reflected the group's recent training in optoelectronic materials, device fabrication, and photonics integration.

Congratulations to the student presenters for representing the group and the college at EMP 2025.
